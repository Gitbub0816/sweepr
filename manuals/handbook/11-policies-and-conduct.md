# 11 · Policies & Code of Conduct

These policies apply to everyone on the platform. Cleaners acknowledge the
relevant ones during [training](./04-training-system.md) (via Acknowledgment
blocks); the binding legal text is served by `apps/legal`.

## Code of conduct (cleaners)

A Sweepr cleaner MUST:

- Treat every home and its occupants with respect and discretion.
- Follow all customer instructions surfaced on arrival (e.g. "avoid nursery",
  "pet-safe products only", "side entrance only").
- Complete the full day-of-service workflow honestly — no staged, reused, or
  off-site photos.
- Keep all communication on the platform; never solicit off-platform work or
  exchange personal contact info.
- Secure the property at checkout and document it.
- Maintain valid coverage and current training at all times.

A Sweepr cleaner MUST NOT:

- Share, photograph for personal use, or harvest customer addresses or access
  codes.
- Perform unauthorized services or bring unapproved persons into a home.
- Falsify location, photos, time, or completion data.

## Damage reporting policy

Damage handling protects both the customer and an honest cleaner.

1. **Report immediately.** Pre-existing damage found during the **before photos**
   inspection MUST be photographed and reported before cleaning begins — this
   protects the cleaner from liability.
2. **Damage during service** MUST be reported via in-app **issue reporting** with
   evidence photos; support is notified automatically.
3. **Customer-reported damage** opens the claims workflow:

   ```text
   Customer reports damage → Create incident → Investigation
   → Determine liability → Submit insurance claim → Resolution
   ```

4. **Liability & coverage** follow [Insurance & Coverage](./06-insurance-and-coverage.md).
   Coverage applies only to approved Sweepr work; it never covers intentional
   damage, criminal activity, or off-platform work.

## Photo & evidence integrity

- Photos MUST be taken on-site, in focus, and well lit; they are timestamped and
  geotagged.
- Reusing, duplicating, or editing evidence photos is a serious violation and is
  detected by AI validation. It can void a payout and result in removal.

## Disputes & re-cleans

- Customers may **report an issue** or **request a re-clean** after completion.
- Payouts tied to an open dispute may be **held** pending resolution
  (see [Payments & Earnings](./08-payments-and-earnings.md)).
- Resolutions are based on the **evidence package** and **communication log** —
  facts over opinions.

## Enforcement

Violations move a cleaner to a restricted state (`paused`) or `rejected`
depending on severity, per the [Cleaner Lifecycle](./03-cleaner-lifecycle.md).
Repeated or severe violations (fraud, safety, criminal activity) result in
permanent removal.

## Customer responsibilities

Customers MUST provide accurate access instructions and a safe working
environment, and MUST use platform communication and dispute channels rather than
off-platform contact.
