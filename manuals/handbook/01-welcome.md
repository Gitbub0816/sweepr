# 01 · Welcome to Sweepr

## Our mission

**Make booking a trustworthy home cleaning as simple, safe, and transparent as
ordering a ride.**

Home cleaning is one of the last large service categories that still runs on
phone tags, cash, no-shows, and zero accountability. Sweepr replaces that with a
modern marketplace where every job is documented, every professional is vetted
and trained, and every customer knows exactly what is happening in their home —
without ever sacrificing privacy.

## Our vision

A world where inviting a professional into your home carries the same confidence
as any other on-demand service: verified identity, real-time tracking,
photographic proof of work, masked communication, and recourse if something goes
wrong.

## What we value

1. **Trust is the product.** Every feature is judged by whether it makes a
   stranger entering someone's home feel safer — for both sides. Privacy
   protections and evidence collection are not add-ons; they are the core.

2. **Document everything, expose nothing unnecessary.** We record timestamps,
   locations, photos, and communications so disputes have facts. We expose
   personal contact details and home addresses to no one until the workflow
   requires it.

3. **Cleaners are professionals, not gig fodder.** We invest in training,
   certification, fair pay, and optional insurance. A respected workforce
   delivers a better customer experience.

4. **Ship the boring, safe thing.** In a business built on trust, a flashy
   feature that leaks an address or skips a verification step is a liability, not
   an asset. We prefer correct over clever.

5. **The handbook is the contract.** If a rule matters, it is written down here
   and, wherever possible, enforced in code.

## How we work

- **Async and written first.** Decisions live in PRs, issues, and this handbook,
  not in someone's memory.
- **Single source of truth.** The database schema, the API, and this handbook
  describe the same reality. When they diverge, that is a bug.
- **Security is everyone's job.** See [Trust & Safety](./07-trust-and-safety.md)
  and [Data, Privacy & Compliance](./09-data-privacy-compliance.md). A short list
  of non-negotiables:
  - Sweepr **NEVER** receives or stores ID images, selfies, or biometric data.
  - Personal phone numbers are **never** exchanged between cleaner and customer.
  - Home addresses and access codes are **withheld** until the workflow unlocks
    them.
